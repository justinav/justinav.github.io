The Process

It all started with a few troubleshooting questions. I did a few quick css patchups before realizing the site needed a complete overhaul.

The first order of business was making the site responsive because it was hard to read Slutist articles on mobile devices. Once I realized that there were 3 overlapping stylesheets and a mess of plugins causing problems, I started working on a new theme design.

After playing with some ideas in Photoshop. I started talking to Kristen (Slutist founder) about priorities and what were the biggest changes she wanted for the site. I built the prototype locally, deployed a development version and after two months of working out key elements, I arrived at the below magazine-style grid layout.
