SEO Driven Updates

  • How many links can we get above the fold? We added modules that could be either programmed by ML or the SEO director
  • We added channel links to each story to strengthen their SEO value

Visual Updates

  • Removed channel-based colors because they confused users and complicated css stylesheets
  • Aligned the entire site to a 1100px, 12 column-grid (an update we were able to make once the site stopped serving skin ads)
  • Condensed text styles, including font-families, colors, sizes, margins, etc
  • Updated the header to reflect Complex's brand, including changing the background to a true #000000 black and utilizing the primary square logo
  • Altered the hero story to fit above the fold and removed html text over images, which gave the Art Department more creative freedom in designing thumbnails

Video Driven Updates

  • We knew 40 shows were on their way to production. How do we make our internet series feel like SHOWS? We worked closely with the Art Department to create placements for show posters. We also created show templates and updated our blog-style video article templates to feel more cinematic.
  • On the homepage we added a Latest Videos playlist that autoplayed on scroll, with modest callouts to our youtube channel, chatbot and apps page.
