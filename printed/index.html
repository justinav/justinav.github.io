Kombucha Workshop

This zine was designed for a two-hour kombucha-making workshop taught by Eliane Gazzard. It all fits on one letter-size sheet of paper. I designed it in Google Slides.

DIY Guide to Band Photos

In 2013, I taught a beginner's photography workshop at Photoville. We started the course by looking at photobooks. Then I presented some of my favorite tips. Afterwards, we all took photos of a live band. I designed this zine because I wanted everyone to have a small reference for all the topics we covered. It's works as a nice pocket guide.

Currently Not On Tour

When I was shooting concerts everday and blogging heavily, I started this zine. I would design and upload my pdfs to magcloud, send out a tweet, and move onto the next one. After a bunch of issues, I wanted to do something collaborative. This ended up being the last issue. To me, it's the best one! It includes amazing work by my friends Karlynn Holland, Kim Kelly, Sam Marble and Vanessa America.
