The Process


It all started over lunch. Mike came up with the concept. He also already had some of the backend developed from another project. We both agreed, we wanted an app that would allow us to just share what we were listening to. I went from sketching some ideas out on paper to drafting wireframes in Google Presentations (my favorite app for collaborative planning).

Once we reviewed all the wireframes together, I started putting together realistic comps in Sketch.
